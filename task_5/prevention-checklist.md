##Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Checklist

#1.UPI Rule of Thumb: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; PINs are only required to send money. 2.  Verify Recipients: Always double-check the VPA (Virtual Payment Address) and the registered name before authorizing any financial transfer. 

#2.Update Software: Keep your phone's OS and banking apps updated to patch security vulnerabilities.

#3.Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking: Never conduct financial transactions or access sensitive accounts over unsecured college or cafe Wi-Fi.

#4.Beware of Urgency: Be skeptical of emails or messages that demand immediate action or threaten negative consequences (classic phishing tactics).

#5.Use Unique Passwords: Use a password manager to ensure you aren't reusing the same password across your college portal and personal accounts.

#6.Limit App Permissions: Regularly audit the permissions (camera, location, contacts) granted to apps on your phone.

#7.Report Incidents Immediately: If compromised, immediately freeze your accounts and report the incident to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
